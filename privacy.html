PERSONAL INFORMATION

 

Information about our Users is an important part of our business and we strive to respect and secure that information as outlined in this Policy.  We do not share your personal information with other organizations for their marketing or promotional purposes without your consent.

 

In order to provide the best User experience, ensure full functionality of our products and services, and otherwise carry out our business activities, we may collect, share and use your personal information as described below.

 

WHAT WE COLLECT

 

We collect information about you in a range of ways.

 

• Information You Give Us.  We collect your‎ name, postal address, email address, phone number, fax number, username, password, demographic information (such as your gender and occupation) as well as other information you directly give us.  In addition, to access certain products or services, we may require additional information, such as date of birth or other verification information.

 

• Information from Use of Products and Services.  We may also collect information through your use of our products and services, including information regarding your usage activity and any other information necessary for us to provide our services, and related billing information.  For Users of our field products, we also collect and maintain data regarding your facilities, including facility name, farm data, field measurements, location, crop types, soil types, production and watering practices.  We also maintain information regarding prior purchases and your inventory of Company products.  For Users who have requested our assistance in establishing a cellular data plan with a cellular carrier, we may also collect and maintain your cellular plan information, including costs and data usage.

 

• Information Automatically Collected.  We automatically log information about you and your computer. For example, when visiting the Site, we log your computer operating system type, IP address, browser type, browser language, the website you visited before browsing to the Site, pages you view, how long you spend on a page, access times and information about your activity on the Site.

 

• Mobile Devices.  If you access the Site or our products and services through a mobile device, we may collect information relating to your mobile device, including phone number, device identifier, type, operating system, or other applications as permitted by the mobile device.  Some of our products and services require or function best when we are able to take into account your location.  Although most mobile devices allow you to turn off location services, you should be aware that the functionality of Company products and services may be limited or unavailable without location services enabled.

 

• Information We Get From Others.  We may get information about you from other sources and may add this to other information we obtain.

 

• Cookies.  We may log information using “cookies.” Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive by a website. We may use both session cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience.  This type of information is collected to make the Site more useful to you and to tailor the experience to meet your interests and needs.

 

WE DO NOT KNOWINGLY COLLECT OR SOLICIT PERSONAL INFORMATION FROM ANYONE UNDER THE AGE OF 18 OR KNOWINGLY ALLOW SUCH PERSONS TO USE THE SITE OR REGISTER FOR AN ACCOUNT (“ACCOUNT”).  IF YOU ARE UNDER 18, DO NOT ATTEMPT TO REGISTER OR SEND US ANY INFORMATION ABOUT YOURSELF.  If we learn that we have collected personal information from anyone under 18 without verification of parental consent, we will attempt to delete such information as soon as possible.  If you are aware that we have any such information or that anyone under 18 has provided us such information, please contact us at the address listed at the end of this Policy.

We strive to provide you with 24/7 access to your Account.  However, outages, equipment failures, updates and maintenance may result in temporary or prolonged periods without access.  We have no obligation to keep your personal information for any particular period and may delete your personal information at any time.  However, in order to provide our services and as a convenience for Users of the Site, we generally anticipate maintaining personal information for at least 2 years.

 

HOW WE SECURE YOUR INFORMATION

 

We take the security of your information seriously and have taken commercially reasonable steps to help ensure that your personal information remains secure.  For all personal information transmitted to or from Company, we use professional data transmission and storage firms that follow industry standards and have experience in the secure transmission and storage of data.

 

In particular, we understand that some Users may have concerns about use of credit cards to purchase Company products and services online.  For orders placed on our Site, we use a technology that automatically encrypts credit card data while information is transmitted over the internet, provided that your browser supports the Secure Socket Layer protocol (SSL).  We do not store your complete credit card number or security code in our records, and only keep the last 4 digits and the expiration date if needed for verification or subsequent purchases.  Only the last 4 digits of your credit card will be displayed on any receipt or purchase confirmation.

 

To protect your privacy and security, we take reasonable steps to verify your identity before granting access to your Account or updating any of your personal information.  In order to assist us, we ask that you protect against unauthorized access of your Account and personal information.  You should never share your password and login information with anyone else.  If you have enabled cookies, browser password retention, or automatic login features, you should protect your electronic device with a secure password so that if your device is lost or stolen your Account will not be compromised.  Be sure to sign off when finished using a shared computer.

 

Despite your and our efforts and any safeguards we may put in place, you should be aware that no form of data storage and transmission, whether in physical or electronic form, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.  Unauthorized access, hardware or software weaknesses or failures, and other factors may compromise the security of personal information.  As a result, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to Company, and you do so at your own risk.  There is also no guarantee that your personal information will not be accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed.  If we learn of a security breach of our data transmission or storage systems we may attempt to notify you electronically or post a notice on the Site so that you can take protective steps.
