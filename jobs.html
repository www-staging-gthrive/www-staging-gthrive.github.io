Senior Software Engineer (JavaScript), April 2015

 

Help feed a hungry planet.  Ag-tech is poised to be one of the hottest growing tech-sectors for the near future and beyond according to San Jose Mercury News .  Here at gThrive we are bringing field analysis of sunlight, moisture, and various soil nutrients to the farmer’s fingertips through today’s and tomorrow’s technology.  Be at the forefront and become part of the gThrive solution to manage and optimize the resources needed to successfully farm in today’s world.  We are looking for someone who is experienced in a full stack environment and who is well versed in software design and development best practices in a high paced environment.

 

Ideally you:

  1. Write production quality JavaScript (5+ years’ experience)
  2. Strongly proficient in CanJS, and JQuery
  3. Are knowledgeable and have used at least one other framework:  AngularJS, Ember, NodeJS, etc.
  4. Solid work experience with HTML5, CSS3, and CSS pre-processors like{ Less}
  5. Have experience with template systems such as Mustache
  6. Know Git and SVN
  7. Have successfully deployed at least one mobile application to iTunes (iPhone) and Google Play Store (Android) preferably with PhoneGap (Cordova)
  8. Full cycle product development experience
  9. Are well acquainted with automated build tools, scripting, and automated test strategies (Grunt, Ant, Travis CI, Selenium or similar)
  10. Understand why we write object oriented code and best practices of architecting an application
  11. BS in Computer Science or related field strongly preferred

 

Pluses:

  1. Have experience writing native plugins for iPhone and Android (Objective-C, Java)
  2. Knowledge of Ruby and some scripting such as Python

 

Responsibilities:

  1. Work in a small, high energy Agile environment sharing best practices, original ideas, and working collaboratively across teams
  2. Work with top performing experienced engineers and product evangelists to bring our product vision to life on our web and mobile applications.
  3. Embrace responsive design and well executed UIs
  4. Be product oriented and thorough in creating, developing and testing applications that are both functional and engaging to our customers.
